Biography

I am an Associate Professor in School of Computer Science and Technology and Institute of Computing and Intelligence at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, working with Prof. Min Zhang.

Before that, I received my PhD degree at University of Macau (2021), advised by Prof. Derek F. Wong and was a member of NLP2CT Lab. I was a postgraduate intern at Tsinghua NLP Group (2018), advised by Prof. Yang Liu and a research intern at Tencent AI Lab (2019-2021), advised by Dr. Zhaopeng Tu and Dr. Longyue Wang.

Looking for Students: Looking for self-motivated students and interns to join our team. Please feel free to contact me if you have interests!
